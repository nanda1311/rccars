Return Policy

RC Rush Website

Thank you for shopping at RC Rush! We strive to provide you with top-quality RC cars and excellent service. Please read our return policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Return Policy Overview

As a small business, we do not offer returns or exchanges on our products. However, we are committed to ensuring that you are satisfied with your purchase. If you have any concerns about your order, please contact our customer support team, and we will do our best to address your issue promptly.

2. Quality Assurance

All our RC cars undergo rigorous quality testing before they are shipped to you. We take pride in the quality and performance of our products and stand behind them. In the rare event that you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately for assistance.

3. Technical Support

We understand that technical issues may arise with your RC car after purchase. Rest assured, we provide dedicated technical support to help you troubleshoot and resolve any problems you may encounter. Our team is here to assist you and ensure that you have a seamless experience with our products.

4. Spare Parts Availability

We offer spare parts support for our pre-existing customers due to limited availability. Spare parts are not available for separate purchase online. If you are an existing customer in need of spare parts, please reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your order or our return policy, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our customer support team is available during business hours, Monday to Friday, from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM (IST).
