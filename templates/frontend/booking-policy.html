Booking Policy

RC Track Consultation – RC Rush

Thank you for booking your consultation with RC Rush!

1. Consultation Fee

The fee for each consultation session is INR 1500, payable at the time of booking. Each session lasts for 60 minutes and can be conducted online or in person, based on the client’s preference.

2. Refund Adjustment Policy

The consultation fee will be adjusted against the total amount if the client makes a purchase of INR 1 lakh or more within 30 days after the consultation. The adjustment will be applied at the time of purchase and reflected in the final invoice.

3. Rescheduling

Clients can reschedule their booking up to 48 hours before the scheduled session without any penalty. If rescheduling occurs after this period, an additional fee of INR 500 may apply, subject to availability.

4. Cancellations

Cancellations made up to 48 hours prior to the scheduled session will receive a 90% refund of the consultation fee. Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance or no-shows will not be eligible for a refund.

5. Payment Methods

All consultation bookings must be paid for in advance via Razorpay.

6. No Obligation to Purchase

While we provide actionable insights during the consultation, there is no obligation to make a purchase after the session.

7. Confidentiality

All discussions during the consultation will remain confidential and will not be shared with third parties without the client’s consent.

8. Limitation of Liability

The consultation is intended to guide business decisions. However, the final responsibility for any decisions made by the client rests with the client.
